Indian.SG

Connecting you with community isn’t just our tagline—it’s our purpose. It’s about building bonds by celebrating cultures and bringing communities together in Singapore.

Our Vision & Purpose

At Indian.SG, we stand as a symbol of cultural exchange and unity. Through fun and creative events, we bring people together, helping different communities in Singapore connect, understand one another better, and celebrate with joy and pride.

Our Ambition

Our goal is to create a friendly, welcoming space where children, youth, parents, and grandparents feel inspired to stay connected to their roots—and play a meaningful role in Singapore’s multicultural journey.

How We Do It

We collaborate with community organisations to run fun and meaningful activities—like storytelling sessions, youth festivals, language festivals, and cultural events.
