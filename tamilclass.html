Preschool Programme • Ages 4–6

Inspire Your Child to Grow Confident in Tamil

Discover Tamil language and culture through play, songs, stories and drama — in a warm, supportive community setting.

“Every child deserves confidence in their mother tongue.”

What children will experience

  • Speech & drama activities (play-based and age-appropriate)
  • Storytelling and listening
  • Pronunciation, enunciation and expressive speaking
  • Creative movement, role-play and simple skits
  • Cultural learning through stories, themes and songs

Focus is on confidence and joy in learning — not performance perfection.
